Busan

Busan, South Korea: South Korea's second largest city, seaside Busan has emerged from the capital's shadow to become a worthy destination of its own. Haeundae Beach: Busan's most famous stretch of sand, Haeundae Beach has a number of popular attractions.

Daegu

Daegu is assuredly a thriving city of music and a key part of the country's overall industry, with 5.6% out of 77,490 nationwide involved, and generating 3.7% of the national music sales. The city has hosted the very first and largest opera festival in Asia, the Daegu International Opera Festival (DIOF).

Gangwon

Gangwon is a famous place for skiing. In Gangwon, there are many mountains and also much snow in winter, so there are many ski resorts in the area. Normally ski resorts open at November and close at March. One of that is Oakvalley ski resort.
